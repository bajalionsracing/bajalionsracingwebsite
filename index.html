Baja Lions Racing

BAJA Lions Racing is Loyola Marymount University's (LMU) official student engineering team that competes in the Baja Series.

Join Us on Leo

Our Mission

We hope to train and educate our members with hands-on experience in design and manufacturing.
Emulate a professional engineering team by following industry best practices and standards.
Promote innovation, collaboration, and excellence within the LMU engineering community.

Our Goals

-----------------------------

Chassis Development

Maintain structural integrity while reducing length to ensure compliance with competition safety rules. Optimize weight distribution to balance manufacturability with performance.

Drivetrain Enhancement

Research ways to optimize the 4WD system to shorten the wheelbase while maintaining performance and reliability.

Transmission Research

We aim to find reliable, compact, and user-friendly options, considering possible alternatives to our current CVT, such as dirtbike transmissions.

Suspension Refinement

Design and tune how the car rides and handles for competition by creating control arms, brackets, and suspension mounts. Work with the chassis team to install shocks, springs, and linkages.
